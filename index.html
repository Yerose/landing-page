Welcome to Ethio-Foods

Welcome to our website showcasing the rich flavors of Ethiopian traditional foods. Discover authentic dishes like injera and doro wat.

Some mouth-watering collection of dishes

"Kitfo" is a delicacy made of raw minced beef, seasoned with spices, and often served with injera and other side dishes.

"Beyaynet" is a platter of various vegetarian dishes served on injera, typically enjoyed during fasting periods.

"Shekla-tibs" is a sizzling hot and spicy beef dish, often served with injera or bread.

"Dorowat" is a spicy and flavorful Ethiopian chicken stew, typically served with injera.

"Gomen-besga" is a delicious combination of collard greens, turmeric, and other spices, usually served with injera or bread.

"Tibs" is a flavorful meat dish, typically served with injera and a variety of side dishes.
